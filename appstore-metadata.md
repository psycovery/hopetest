Full Description (4,000 chars max) [EDIT]:
---
Hope Forward is a fresh start in your pocket.

Powered by Psycovery — specialists in Hope Theory and forensic mental health —
Hope Forward walks alongside you as you rebuild your life after the criminal
justice system. Whether you're newly released, on licence, or supporting
someone on their journey, this app gives you the tools to move forward.

─ WHAT IS HOPE THEORY? ─
Hope Theory is a proven psychological framework built on three pillars:
• Goals — knowing where you want to go
• Pathways — finding the route to get there
• Agency — believing you can do it

Hope Forward brings all three to life in a simple, supportive app.

─ FEATURES ─

🎯 Goal Library
Browse specialist goals across 8 life areas: Housing, Employment, Education,
Family, Health, Finance, Probation & Supervision, and Identity. Each goal
comes with a ready-made 3-step pathway to get you started.

🛤️ My Pathways
Track your progress step by step. Check off completed actions and watch your
Hope Score grow.

📍 Local Services Directory
Find housing, employment, mental health, substance use, legal, family, food,
and community services near you — searchable by town or city across the UK.

🏆 Hope Leaderboard
Join a supportive community of people on similar journeys. See how your Hope
Score compares and draw strength from others' progress.

📈 My Progress
Visualise your journey with a personal Hope Score, community comparison, and
goal-by-goal progress tracking.

🌅 Psycovery Coaching
Connect with specialist 1:1 coaches trained in Hope Theory and experienced in
forensic and reentry settings.

🆘 Emergency Support
Instant access to free, confidential 24/7 crisis helplines — always one tap
away.

─ ABOUT PSYCOVERY ─
Hope Forward is built and powered by Psycovery, founded by David
Adlington-Rivers. Psycovery applies Hope Theory in forensic settings, offering
specialist coaching, mental health support, and criminal justice reform
consultancy across the UK.

Visit: www.psycovery.co.uk

Your journey starts now. 🌅

Notes for Apple Review Team:
---
Hope Forward is a mental health and reentry support app for people rebuilding
their lives after the criminal justice system. It is powered by Psycovery
(www.psycovery.co.uk), a UK-based organisation specialising in Hope Theory
and forensic mental health.

The app does NOT require an account to use. All core features (goal library,
pathways, directory, progress tracking) are available immediately on launch.

The Emergency Support screen contains links to legitimate UK crisis helplines
(Samaritans, Mind, Shelter, Frank) — these are informational only and comply
with Apple's guidelines for apps that reference mental health resources.

The coaching booking feature (Psycovery Coaching screen) directs users to
psycovery.co.uk for external booking — no in-app purchase is involved.

There is no user-generated content shared publicly. The leaderboard uses
pseudonymised Hope Scores only — no personal data is exposed.
